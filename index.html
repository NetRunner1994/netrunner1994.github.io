Common Questions

Everything you want to know before reaching out.

Do I have to bring my device anywhere?▾
Nope. I come to you. If you're in North Texas I'll show up at your home or office. If you're farther out, we can handle most things remotely — I'll walk you through it over the phone or connect to your device securely from my end.
How much does it cost?▾
Everything has a flat price listed in the pricing section above. No hourly rate, no hidden fees. I'll confirm the price with you before I start anything — you never get a surprise bill.
What if you can't fix it?▾
You don't pay. Simple as that. If I can't solve the problem I won't charge you for my time. The only exception is the $50 diagnostic fee for devices that won't turn on — that covers the time it takes to figure out what's wrong.
How do I pay?▾
Cash, Venmo, Zelle, or CashApp — whatever is easiest for you. Payment is collected after the job is done and you're satisfied.
Do you work on weekends?▾
Yes. I work 7 days a week including weekends. Same-day appointments are often available — just reach out and we'll figure out a time that works for you.
Is my personal information safe?▾
Absolutely. I never access anything I don't need to, never store your personal files, and never share your information. Your privacy is taken seriously on every single job.
How fast will you respond to my request?▾
Usually within 2 hours during the day. Fill out the form, send a text, or call — and I'll get back to you fast with a price and available times.
Do you work on Macs and iPhones too?▾
Yes. I work on Windows PCs, laptops, Macs, iPhones, Android phones, tablets, and most home devices. If you're not sure, just ask — chances are I can help.
