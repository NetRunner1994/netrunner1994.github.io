Upgrade Services

What We Can Upgrade

💥
GPU Swap
See exact pricing below ↓
What it is: Pulling your old graphics card and installing a new one, then updating the drivers — the software that lets your PC and the new card talk to each other — and confirming everything is stable.
Why you might need it: Probably the single biggest upgrade for gaming or video work. If games feel slow or look blurry, this is usually the fix.
🧠
RAM Upgrade
See exact pricing below ↓
What it is: Adding more memory or swapping in faster sticks.
Why you might need it: One of the cheapest fixes there is. If your PC slows down or freezes when you have a lot open at once, this is usually why.
💾
SSD Installation
See exact pricing below ↓
What it is: Swapping your old spinning hard drive for a solid-state drive.
Why you might need it: Boot up in seconds, games and programs load almost instantly. If your PC takes forever to start, this is the upgrade.
🔧
Full System Refresh
See exact pricing below ↓
What it is: Full teardown, dust out, fresh thermal paste, cables redone, drivers updated.
Why you might need it: Makes a real difference on machines that haven’t been touched in years and are running hot or loud.
